Market-Based Solutions

Remove regulatory barriers, streamline permitting, reduce fees that drive up the cost of new construction.

ADU & Infill Development

Support accessory dwelling units to increase housing supply without suburban sprawl.

First-Time Buyer Support

Down payment assistance and state-level incentive programs to help families achieve homeownership.

Anti-Mandate

Opposes state-mandated density; supports local control with smart incentives that respect community character.

Workforce Housing

Ensure teachers, firefighters, and nurses can afford to live where they work and serve our communities.
